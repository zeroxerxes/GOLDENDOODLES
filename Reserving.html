RESERVING YOUR PUPPY


Our puppies are sold on a spay/neuter contract only and there is a screening process. When I receive your application, I will give you a call so we can chat about the process, our puppies, time frame of puppy availability, and expectations. When an individual or family places a deposit with us, their name will go on our general waitlist. Our waitlist page is password protected and available for viewing by deposited families only. When a litter is born, we will contact those on this list (in order of deposit received) to see if they would like to commit to the litter just born or if they would like to remain on the general list and be called when the next litter is born.  This commitment is to the litter and not a specific puppy - families choose their puppy in person at eight weeks. In each litter, Goldendoodles Forever® may be holding puppies back for our program and reserve the right to do so at any time. Prices quoted upon placing your reservation are good for one year and then are subject to change.  To reserve your puppy, please head over to our application page to begin the process.
