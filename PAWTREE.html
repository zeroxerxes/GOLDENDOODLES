I have been feeding dogs all of my life. And, in that time, my family (and then me as an adult), have fed about every kind of food out there. Three years ago I switched to pawTree. I was looking for a smaller kibble for my puppies; more variety of flavors; no recalls; human grade ingredients. I'm a researcher so I did my homework and chose pawTree. ​pawTree offers premium quality food, treats and supplements made with only the finest ingredients. They have an extremely helpful website that allows one to shop by condition (like dry skin or elderly or over/under weight) or simply by flavor. My retired girls get turkey and rice now since their metabolism is slowing with age. My mommas and puppies get chicken and oatmeal. If I have a new mom that is being finicky after giving birth, I use the super food toppers to give them some extra flavor and additional nutrients. Your pets are a part of your family, and pawTree (and I) want them to not just survive but to thrive. pawTree continues to raise the bar of the quality of products developed in the industry. They refuse to compromise on quality, so you don't have to. pawTree’s mission is to make such a profound difference in the lives of pets and their people that they can't imagine going back to the old way. They have succeeded for me - like my decision, after getting my first Goldendoodle, to never own another breed, I can't imagine a time when I will not choose pawTree for my dogs.
