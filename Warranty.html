Our Warranty

The Seller warrants the puppy is in good health at the time the Buyer assumes ownership. Buyer agrees to have the puppy examined by a licensed veterinarian within 3 days of receipt. If, as a result of the examination, the veterinarian determines that the puppy is not in good health, the cause of which is clearly attributable to the Seller, the Buyer may return the puppy, along with a written diagnosis from said veterinarian, for a full refund of the purchase price.

Failure to have the puppy examined by a licensed veterinarian will nullify this warranty. If the Buyer chooses to keep the puppy, no replacement will be given and all medical costs and treatments costs will be the sole responsibility of the Buyer. Seller will not provide a refund in the event of any abuse or neglect after the Buyer assumes ownership.  

The Seller warrants that the parents are free from any known hip or shoulder dysplasia. If the puppy develops symptomatic hip or shoulder dysplasia, verified with x-ray exam, within the first two years, the seller will pay the related veterinarian expenses up to the purchase price, provided that a veterinarian can confirm, in writing, that the condition is not caused by the puppy being overweight and/or having been over-exercised.  

The Seller warrants the puppy will be free from the following DNA-testable disorders: CDDY, CDPA, DM, GR-PRA 1, GR-PRA 2, Ich, MD, NCL-GR, NEwS, prcd-PRA, vWD1. If, within the first 2 years from purchase, the puppy is diagnosed with any of the above disorders and such disorder will effect the puppy’s life-expectancy and/or enjoyment of life, Seller will pay the related veterinarian expenses up to the purchase price.

Diagnosis and prognosis must be confirmed, in writing, by a licensed veterinarian and Seller reserves the right to have the dog’s DNA tested before compensation is given.   The Seller makes no warranties, nor guarantees, that the puppy is an allergy-free dog, hypo-allergenic, or non-shedding.
